# Department Leader Style

Use this style for leaders who are more result-oriented and less focused on low-level implementation detail.

## Tone

- concise
- business-facing
- action-oriented
- confident but not overstated

## Emphasis

Prioritize in this order:

1. business-relevant result
2. high-level direction and value
3. current risk or blocker
4. next-step ownership and momentum

## Detail level

Include:

- what improved or regressed
- why the result matters to real workloads or delivery goals
- what you are doing next
- where leadership support may help

Reduce:

- low-level mechanism discussion
- tool-chain nuances
- detailed experiment chronology

## Suggested phrasing

Prefer phrases such as:

- "本轮结果表明"
- "对后续业务/交付的意义是"
- "当前主要问题集中在"
- "下一步会优先推进"
- "如需进一步支持，建议"
